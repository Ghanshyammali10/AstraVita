Frequently Asked Questions

How can I organize a blood donation event?

Contact our events team through the "Host an Event" button above. We'll provide you with all necessary resources and support.

What are the requirements for volunteering?

Volunteers must be 18 or older, able to commit to at least 4 hours, and attend a brief orientation session.

How long does a typical blood donation event last?

Most events run for 4-6 hours, but larger community drives may last all day.
